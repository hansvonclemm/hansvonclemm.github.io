Lightfoot Electric

Otherlab | CA

The Project

We were tasked to design and prototype a wheelchair accessible electric vehicle that could fit within a 1.3 x 2 x 1.4 meter box.

The vehicle we designed had several unique features, of note was an air suspension system that allowed the floor of the vehicle to 'kneel' down, allowing hassle-free wheelchair access. The vehicle was also equipped with a full 500W solar roof with the ability to provide 15 miles of range per day charged.

My Role

After just one year of engineering training, I supported the team in any way I could. This included:

  • Rapidly prototyping many design ideas using a variety of techniques (laser cutting, waterjet, etc.)
